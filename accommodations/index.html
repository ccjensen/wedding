Accommodations

We have compiled a list of accommodation suggestions together with our wedding planner who operates in Cinque Terre. If you decide to book at any of the ones suggested, make sure to mention that you were referred by Melina Nicholson from “Cinque Terre Wedding” and will be attending our wedding at Boronco Winery. This should guarantee that you are well looked after.

Melina suggested The Net Accommodation Service, which is a one-stop shop for accommodation in the area, as an easier option than contacting hotels individually as they cover a range of accommodation options and prices. Here you fill out an accommodation request form with your requirements and they will check availabilites for you.
